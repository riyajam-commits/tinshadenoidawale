FAQ

Everything You Need to Know

Do you provide custom shed solutions?

Yes. All our sheds and roofing structures are custom-built on-site based on your location, usage, and size requirements.

What is the approximate cost of a tin shed?

Approximate rates: Tin Shed ₹150–250/sq ft, Warehouse ₹200–350/sq ft, MS Structure ₹180–300/sq ft. Free site visit for exact quote.

How long does a project take to complete?

Small shed (upto 1000 sq ft): 7–10 days. Medium shed: 15–20 days. Large warehouse: 25–35 days. MS Structure: 10–15 days.

Do you offer free site visits?

Yes! We provide completely FREE site visits including measurement, material recommendation, and exact cost estimation. Call +91-8527977714.

Which areas do you serve?

We serve all of Noida, Greater Noida, Ghaziabad, Faridabad, Gurugram, Delhi and the entire NCR region.

What materials do you use?

We use only ISI certified steel and roofing sheets with rust-proof treatment ensuring 5+ years of durability.

Will the structure withstand heavy rain?

Yes. Our sheds are designed with proper slope, drainage, and material selection to handle Indian weather conditions.

How can I get started?

Simply call or WhatsApp us at +91-8527977714 or fill the contact form below. We'll guide you with the right structure and cost estimate.
