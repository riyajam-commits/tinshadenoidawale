About Us

Welcome to Tin Shade Noida

Tin Shade Noida is a leading manufacturer and installer of tin sheds, MS structures, and industrial roofing solutions. Serving clients across Noida, Greater Noida, and the entire NCR region for over 15 years.

We specialize in factory sheds, warehouse roofing, parking sheds, and heavy-duty MS fabrication. Our experienced team delivers every project on time with the highest quality standards.

  • Premium grade ISI certified steel & roofing sheets
  • Free site visit & detailed cost estimation
  • On-time project delivery — guaranteed
  • 5+ year durability with rust-proof treatment
